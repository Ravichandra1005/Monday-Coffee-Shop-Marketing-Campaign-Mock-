## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this mock campaign is to increase **brand awareness** and **drive website traffic** by targeting coffee lovers in key Indian cities — **Pune**, **Delhi**, and **Jaipur** — using Facebook and Instagram ads.

**Campaign Type:** Facebook Traffic Campaign  
**Target Platforms:** Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Instagram Stories  
**Primary Goal:** Website Clicks + Brand Recall

---

## ⚙️ Facebook Ads Setup

**Steps to create the campaign:**

1. Log in to [Meta Business Suite](https://business.facebook.com/)
2. Click **Create Ad** → Choose **Traffic** as the campaign objective
3. Enter your **website URL**
4. Connect your **Facebook Page** and **Instagram Account**
5. Select **Manual Placements** → Enable:
   - Facebook Feed
   - Instagram Feed
   - Instagram Stories

## 📊 Tracking & Optimization

### ✅ Key Metrics to Monitor:
- CTR (Click-Through Rate)
- CPC (Cost Per Click)
- Website Traffic via Meta Pixel
- Engagement: Likes, Shares, Saves, Comments

### 🛠 Setup Tips:
- Install **Meta Pixel** on your website
- Use **Meta Ads Manager** for:
  - Custom reports
  - Campaign breakdowns
  - Audience insights
- Monitor and adjust:
  - Pause low CTR ads
  - Boost top performers
  - Test new creative weekly
